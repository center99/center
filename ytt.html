YTT with Yogakshema

YTT - Yoga teachers training is the six months weekend course to get trained as a yoga teacher. The certificate will be issued after clearing the examination. Training covers famous yoga veteran Sri Dr. BKS Iyengar's way of yoga practices. The course is beneficial for both beginners and experienced yoga practitioners. Towards the end of the course, you will be expertise in asanas postures, pranayama practice, and dhyana along with public speaking skills, teaching methodologies.

YTT Batches achieving blessings from Guruji.

Anyone interested for this particular training can contact us!
